COMMUNITY SERVICE PROJECT – JASMINE PLANTS (Day 1 to 6 Report)

Day Date Activity Observations / Notes
Day 1 03-06-2024 Conducted survey on Jasmine plants in an area Came to know that many people use pesticides in their jasmine gardens.
Day 2 04-06-2024 Planted a jasmine plant with pesticides Noticed that pesticides are commonly used for jasmine gardens.
Day 3 05-06-2024 Survey on jasmine garden using cow dung Realised that cow dung is very useful for growing healthy jasmine plants.
Day 4 06-06-2024 Survey on jasmine plants grown in front of houses Learned practical methods for growing jasmine plants at home.
Day 5 07-06-2024 Asked people about organic farming processes Realised that jasmine gardens grow healthier with organic farming techniques.
Day 6 08-06-2024 Learned about using organic fertilizers for plant growth Noticed that many people still use chemical pesticides instead of organic fertilizers.
