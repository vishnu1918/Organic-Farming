COMMUNITY SERVICE PROJECT – Week 6 Report

Day Date Activity
Day 1 Conducted survey on Jasmine plants in an area Came to know that many people use pesticides in their jasmine gardens.
Day 2 Planted a jasmine plant with pesticides Noticed that pesticides are commonly used for jasmine gardens.
Day 3 Survey on jasmine garden using cow dung Realised that cow dung is very useful for growing healthy jasmine plants.
Day 4 Survey on jasmine plants grown in front of houses Learned practical methods for growing jasmine plants at home.
Day 5 Asked people about organic farming processes Realised that jasmine gardens grow healthier with organic farming techniques.
Day 6 Learned about using organic fertilizers for plant growth Noticed that many people still use chemical pesticides instead of organic fertilizers.
